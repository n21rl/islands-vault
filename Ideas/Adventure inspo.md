

## Colonial & Cultural Conflict

- **Ruins of Azlant (Pathfinder)** – Lost colony mystery in an archipelago setting; undersea ruins and ancient powers
- **Wages of Vice (Radiant Citadel)** – Urban colonial inequity and spiritual resistance during a festival
- **Strange New World** – Weird fantasy colonial sandbox
- **Northern Crown: New World Adventures** – Magical 17th-century Americas
- **A True Relation of The Virginia Disastrum** – Jamestown colony horror
- **Colonial Gothic** – Supernatural colonial intrigue
- **Flames of Freedom: Beginnings** – Revolutionary era campaign starter
