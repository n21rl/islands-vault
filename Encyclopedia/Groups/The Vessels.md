The Vessels of De La Vasque are the remnants of his grand experiment—souls excised, reforged, and trapped within grotesque reliquaries of flesh and faith. Some are still bound to fractured purpose, others barely clinging to awareness. They bear names given by their creator. 

Depaille - Scarecrow
Cogsworth - Clock
Lumière - Candle
Forte - Organ
Fierabras - Armour
