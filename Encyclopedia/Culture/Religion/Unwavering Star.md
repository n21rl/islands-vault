The Unwavering Star is a large celestial body that appeared in the sky more than 1000 years ago. It is a dark spot around which ribbons of purplish mists shimmer. Unlike other stars, it is present day and night, and it does not seem to move.
It is revered as the one true god by the people of Steorr. It spurred their unification and later colonial expansion, seen as their guiding light.
The Church of the Unwavering Star preaches values of order, hard work, and spreading of the faith.
