The Jawist unity symbol is is composed of 3 concentric circles of increasing size. From the center, 3 lines extend outward, dividing the circles into thirds and extending a short distance past the outwrmost circle. An old symbol, it has been repurposed into modern political emblem, representing a call for contemporary intertribal solidarity rather than a lost cultural past.
