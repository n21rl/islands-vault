Captain Morgan is the leader of [[The Drunks]], one of the founders of the [[Republic|Republic]] and its most powerful captain. He is a charismatic and cunning man who was once a privateer for the [[Steorr|Steorrian Crown]], raiding [[Selvea (city)|Selvean]] targets. He turned to piracy after the end of the war, feeling betrayed by his former employer. He is a master of strategy and tactics, and has a reputation for being ruthless and daring.

He is wearing a red wide-brimmed hat with a gold trim over a white bandana on his head. A has a blue cape. He wears an ornate red cloak with a gold trim and a ruffled white shirt poking out of it. His face looks confident. He has shoulder length black hair, a moustache slightly upucurled at the ends, and a thin goatee (style sometimes referred to as imperial). He wears a golden hoop earing on each ear. A brown leather belt is strapped across his chest
